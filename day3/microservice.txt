A big service is broken down into a small number of independent services.
Each service should do one particular task or business logic.

Communication happens with other services with the help of APIs.

1. Small and focused.
2. Independent deployment
3. Interaction is thru APIs.

Advantages:
- Increased Scalability.
- Improved Fault Tolerance.
- Faster deployment.
- Different tools and different lang for different services.

For example:
User Service: Manages the user account(sign up management)

Product services: Product information, Inventory, Pricing.

Notification Service: Email, SMS 

Shopping Cart Service: Managing the items in the cart, cart data persistence etc.

Content Management Services: Blogs, Post, Images.
